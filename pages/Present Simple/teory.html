Present Simple

Present Simple Tense

We use Present Simple to talk about things, that someone do ussually (every day, morning, always, never...)

Affirmative

  • I + Verb
  • You + Verb
  • We + Verb
  • They + Verb
  • He or Verb+s
  • She or Verb+s
  • It or Verb+s

Negative

  • I do + not + Verb
  • You do + not + Verb
  • We do + not + Verb
  • They do + not + Verb
  • He does + not + Verb
  • She does + not + Verb
  • It does + not + Verb

Interrogative

  • Do I + Verb
  • Do You + Verb
  • Do We + Verb
  • Do They + Verb
  • Does He + Verb
  • Does She + Verb
  • Does It + Verb

When we talking about he/she/it we always add "s" to the ending of verb, BUT if it an interrogative or negative sentence we don't add "s" ending
