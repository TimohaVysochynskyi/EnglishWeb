Emergency calls

There are a lot of accidents in every pearson's life and always we need to call emergency services. And in these little list I will describe, what have you to do in the accidents.

Medical help

Firstly, you have to phone 103 in Ukraine or 112 in other European countries. Then, say the adress of an accident and describe all details. REMEMBER! The dispatcher finishes the call!
Secondly, move the pearson to the green zone. When you are waiting for help, you have to protect the victim and don't allow people to do something with him. Then listen to the breath and count it for 10 seconds, if it's bleeding, take gloves and a mask, then put compression bandage and strongly move the part of a body NOT HEAD!
After that, take an assistant and meet the ambulance, also you have to say to use a resuscitation or CPR systems if pearson is without breath.
Finally, undress your gloves, but very tidely, because blood can touch your hands and you can die. Don't forget to wash your hands and don't touch eyes and your mouth.
